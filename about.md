## Menu Translate

Snap a menu photo for instant AI translation in 9 languages. Get cultural context, pronunciation help, and visual order sheets. Never guess what you're ordering again!

Nothing humbles a traveler faster than a local-language menu with zero translations. You sit down hungry and confident, ready for a feast, and five minutes later you're sweating over what might be chicken or absolutely not chicken. Meanwhile, the locals next to you order and their table overflows with dishes you didn't even know existed—the kind you meant to try but never had the words for.

Menu Translate breaks down language barriers so you can enjoy authentic dining experiences anywhere in the world.

### KEY FEATURES

• AI-Powered Translation
Snap a photo of any menu and watch it transform into your language in seconds. Our advanced AI technology works with any menu layout—vertical or horizontal text, multiple columns, or complex designs. Just point and shoot.

• 9 Languages Supported
Translate menus to English, Japanese, Korean, Chinese (Simplified & Traditional), French, Spanish, German, Italian, and more. Your language, your way.

• Rich Information
Get cultural context, detailed explanations, and insights about each dish to make informed choices. Understand ingredients, cooking methods, and regional specialties before you order.

• Easy Ordering
Order confidently with visual order sheets. Build your perfect meal and show it to your server. No more pointing at random items or hoping for the best.

• Pronunciation Help
Learn how to pronounce dish names correctly. Speak confidently when ordering and connect with local culture.

• Smart Photo Processing
Works seamlessly with any menu format. Our intelligent system recognizes text in various layouts and orientations, ensuring accurate translations every time.

PERFECT FOR
• Travelers exploring new cuisines
• Food enthusiasts discovering authentic dishes
• Anyone dining at restaurants with foreign-language menus
• People who want to make informed food choices

### HOW IT WORKS
1. Open Menu Translate
2. Take a photo of the menu (or select from your gallery)
3. Get instant translation in your preferred language
4. Explore dish details, cultural context, and pronunciation
5. Build your order with visual order sheets
6. Show your order to the server and enjoy!

Menu Translate transforms every dining experience from a guessing game into an adventure. Great trips deserve great food, not random plates that leave you feeling like you watched everyone else enjoy the real show while you missed the main act.

Download Menu Translate today and never miss out on the perfect dish again.

## About
Monochrome is a minimal, responsive theme created for Jekyll. The goal of Monochrome is to provide a ready to use, plug and play theme for those who want to set up a blog, and focus solely on the content.

## Features

- Completely responsive and mobile first
- Clean SEO friendly URLs, auto-generated from post title (no messy dates in the url)
- SEO title/description integration
- Sitemap ready
- Easy customization for header, footer, navigation links, colors, favicon etc
- Default Monochrome Color Palette - black, white, greys

### Setup

Monochrome may be installed by simply downloading the .zip folder from the [repository on Github](https://github.com/thereviewindex/monochrome/archive/master.zip).

After extracting the content from the folder into the selected directory, you can type ``jekyll serve`` from the terminal, than open your browser to ``0.0.0.0:4000/monochrome/`` and you will find it there.

Additionally it is possible to fork the repository and use Github Pages as hosting. By following this way it will be enough to change the ``baseurl`` value into the ``_config.yml`` file, with the directory name of your project (for example /blog) or simply with a "/" (slash) if you want install Monochrome in the root. 



For further details on Monochrome, please visit the [repository on Github](https://github.com/thereviewindex/monochrome/).
