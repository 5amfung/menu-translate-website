## Menu Translate

Snap a menu photo for instant AI translation in 9 languages. Get cultural context, pronunciation help, and visual order sheets. Never guess what you're ordering again!

Nothing humbles a traveler faster than a local-language menu with zero translations. You sit down hungry and confident, ready for a feast, and five minutes later you're sweating over what might be chicken or absolutely not chicken. Meanwhile, the locals next to you order and their table overflows with dishes you didn't even know existed—the kind you meant to try but never had the words for.

Menu Translate breaks down language barriers so you can enjoy authentic dining experiences anywhere in the world.

### KEY FEATURES

• AI-Powered Translation
Snap a photo of any menu and watch it transform into your language in seconds. Our advanced AI technology works with any menu layout—vertical or horizontal text, multiple columns, or complex designs. Just point and shoot.

• 9 Languages Supported
Translate menus to English, Japanese, Korean, Chinese (Simplified & Traditional), French, Spanish, German, Italian, and more. Your language, your way.

• Rich Information
Get cultural context, detailed explanations, and insights about each dish to make informed choices. Understand ingredients, cooking methods, and regional specialties before you order.

• Easy Ordering
Order confidently with visual order sheets. Build your perfect meal and show it to your server. No more pointing at random items or hoping for the best.

• Pronunciation Help
Learn how to pronounce dish names correctly. Speak confidently when ordering and connect with local culture.

• Smart Photo Processing
Works seamlessly with any menu format. Our intelligent system recognizes text in various layouts and orientations, ensuring accurate translations every time.
